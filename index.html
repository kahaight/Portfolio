About Me

I was a researcher and data analyst for 10 years with a focus on juvenile and adult criminal justice system practices. I decided that I would be happier as a computer programmer and took the steps necessary to make that possible, with the help of Eleven Fifty Academy, the Pathfinder program, and the support of my wife. I started building my skills in .NET development at Eleven Fifty. I then acquired my Platform Developer I certification shortly after graduating from the Pathfinder program. Since then I have continued my work as a researcher while also working as a learning assistant at Eleven Fifty, where I assist others in beginning their software development journeys.
